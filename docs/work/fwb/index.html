Farmers Without Borders

Online selling point where farmers can sell there products to customers in the city.

view application
Challenge

Our goal is to connect small local farmers and producers to urban communities by providing a fresh and local alternative to big chain grocery outlets. We have a wide selection of local produce that is delivered right to your doorstep. There’s no longer a barrier to good and healthy food. We believe that organic and local produce is for everyone. You deserve to eat better. This assignment was in association with students from Seneca College based in Toronto, Canada.
